Introduction

Walking through the world of SCP

What's this?

  • A wiki for those who want to check the SCPs and tales in which their favorite SCPs appear.
  • You can also enjoy the visualization of SCP network here.

Data collection

  • The data were collected from SCP Foundation on February 17, 2021.
  • The list of tales for each SCP is made by scanning all the tales in Tales by Title and scraping SCP Tales by Series.
